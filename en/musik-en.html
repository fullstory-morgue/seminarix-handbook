Seminarix is a Live-medium based on sidux, which is developed for the education of teachers

  Section Music

Notes

The programs for musical education are naturally also usable during the lessons.

We offer here, some very professional tools for editing audio files.

Programs Introduction

The programs can be started/installed with the help of the program-starter. The programs of this page can be found under the menu item "Music". You can find the program-starter in the "Kde-Menu" in the sub-menu "Utilities", or you can just click on the program-starter icon on the desktop.

Amarok

A popular audio player for Linux.

Amarok

Audacity

Audio-Editor, Open-Source Software.

Available for Windows, Mac (OS 9 and OS X) and Linux. This is a flexible audio program which does not have to be afraid of being compared to proprietary software.

A real alternative for in-school recording and editing with zero cost.

MIDI-Import, virtual multi-track technique, Undo-Function and working with various formats and sampling rates allow for a professional result.

Audacity

Glame

LINUX-Audio-Editor, with extensive editing possibilities. Emphasis is given on editing with various effects, creation of new effects by combining so-called building blocks.

An interesting program, which is from an educational point of view mostly suited for work-groups in the area of sound technology.

Glame

Hydrogen

Hydrogen is a virtual drum-computer and sequencer.

Hydrogen combines an easy pattern oriented programming with professional functions like steering via MIDI and freely definable samples. In Linux, Hydrogen is compatible to the JACK sound-server.

Hydrogen

Juk

JuK is a Jukebox-Program written especially for KDE. JuK is mostly used to manage audio files but of course it can also play them. It supports MP3, Ogg Vorbis and FLAC audio files.

Juk

NoteEdit

NoteEdit is an Open-Source musical score editor. It offers the following functions:

  • Support of polyphonic voices of unlimited number and length.
  • MIDI-rendering of the written notes, chords, texts.
  • It contains a number of export and import filters for various formats like MIDI, MusicXML, ABC, MUP, PMX, MusiXTeX αnd LilyPond.
  • Graphical interface for note inputting and print preview of those, which you can later optimize with the powerful score program LilyPond (it gets installed automatically with NoteEdit) or with MusiXTeX.
  • Reading and rendering from a MIDI-Keyboard
  • Automatic set-up of measure lines when a measure is given and more.

NoteEdit

solfege

solfege is a music training program, which you can use to train your rhythm, interval and chords knowledge. Besides that you can read theoretical chapters for various subjects or test your knowledge with practical hearing tests.

If there is no sound, please install solfege-oss!

solfege is also available for other Operating Systems.
