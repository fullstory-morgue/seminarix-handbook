Seminarix is a Live-medium based on aptosid, which is developed for the education of teachers.

  E-Learning Section

Notes

Naturally in this section you can mostly find programs and program groups which stimulate and help organise the cooperative learning process.

You can find a lot of them, especially under Linux. Usually for this purpose data-bank applications have to be installed and maintained.

This is in practice not possible on a Live-medium.

Because of that you will not find any program from this category on the Live-medium.

Should you be interested in setting up such a learning environment in a computer network, it would be wise to have an expert by your side.

Should you want to contact the people in charge for this project, please email us.
For non-technical questions Email info@seminarix.org,
For technical questions Email edu@sidux-ev.de
