Seminarix is a Live-medium based on sidux, which is developed for the education of teachers

  Religion Section

Notes

For the subject of "Religion", there are various interesting programs like Bibletime, hebcal and itools.

Programs Introduction

The programs can be started/installed with the help of the program-starter. The programs of this page can be found under the menu item "Religion". You can find the program-starter in the "Kde-Menu" in the sub-menu "Utilities", or you can just click on the program-starter icon on the desktop.

BibleTime

BibleTime is the Bible in an electronic format and makes the work in religion class much easier. The integrated search function provides very good services. The Bible is available in various languages, commentaries and lexicons can be installed afterwards. Numerous Language Bibles are easily usable with Bibletime like the Luther-bible from 1545 and the Elberfelder Bibles from 1871 and 1905. Even the knowledge of a priest is installable.

The many extensions (translations, commentaries, and lexicons (Greek and Hebrew are also available)) can be installed to the hard disk after the installation of Seminarix.

hebcal

Hebcal is a command line program which prints out, for a given Gregorian year, the days in the Jewish calendar. Hebcal provides flexiblity for showing the events in the Jewish calendar year.

hebcal  |   Info hebcal

itools

itools is a collection of command line tools. It contains Islamic Hijri calendars and prayer time tools. The following commands are available:
* ical: Displays a Hijri calendar.
* idate: is an extensive Hijri/Gregorian date converter.
* ipraytime: Prayer times and Qibla calculator and also a schedule table generator.
* ireminder: Prayer time reminder.
